## Backup Plans

### When Cafe WiFi Fails

1. **Mobile data** - Get a local SIM with data. Globe and Smart both have good coverage in Angeles.
2. **Pocket WiFi** - Rent one from the airport or buy prepaid
3. **Hotel lobbies** - Some hotels have work areas with reliable WiFi
4. **Coworking spaces** - There are a few in Clark (see below)

### Coworking Spaces in Clark

If you need serious work infrastructure:

- **Clock In** - Proper coworking setup with meeting rooms
- **Various serviced offices** - More formal, daily/monthly rates

## Cafe Etiquette for Remote Workers

1. **Buy something every 2 hours** - At minimum. Don't nurse one coffee for 6 hours.
2. **Don't take up a 4-person table** - If you're solo, sit accordingly
3. **Use headphones for calls** - Nobody wants to hear your Zoom meeting
4. **Peak hours = time to go** - If lunch rush hits and there's a line, pack up
5. **Tip the staff** - Especially if you're camping out

---

## Power Tips

- Bring a power strip - One outlet becomes three
- Battery backup - For those cafes with no outlets
- Sit strategically - Scout outlets before ordering
- Morning is best - Cafes are emptier, outlets are available
