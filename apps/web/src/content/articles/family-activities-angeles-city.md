# Family Activities in Angeles City & Clark

Angeles City and Clark aren't just for adults. Whether you're living here with kids or visiting with family, there's actually a lot to do that doesn't involve the nightlife scene everyone talks about.

Here's your guide to keeping the whole family entertained.

### Aqua Planet
**Location:** Clark Freeport Zone  
**Price:** ~‚Ç±900-1200/person (check for promos)  
**Best For:** Hot days, water lovers, bigger kids

The biggest water park in the region. Wave pools, slides, lazy rivers, and enough activities to fill a full day. Get there when it opens to maximize slide time before lines build up.

**Tips:**
- Rent a cabana if you can‚Äîworth it for shade and storage
- Bring waterproof bags for phones/valuables

### Zoocobia Fun Zoo
**Location:** Clark Freeport Zone  
**Price:** ~‚Ç±495/person  
**Best For:** Animal encounters, younger kids

A more hands-on zoo experience where kids can feed and interact with animals. They have goats, rabbits, birds, and various other critters. Also has a Gravity Car ride that's fun for older kids and adults.

**Tips:**
- The Gravity Car has height/age restrictions‚Äîcheck before promising kids
- Bird feeding is a hit with little ones
- It's smaller than expected; 2-3 hours is enough

### Nayong Pilipino Clark
**Location:** Clark Freeport Zone  
**Price:** ~‚Ç±300/person  
**Best For:** Cultural education, school field trips

A cultural park showcasing different regions of the Philippines through replica villages. It's educational and a good way for kids (and adults) to learn about Filipino heritage. It's quiet, not crowded, and there's a lot of space to walk around.

**Tips:**
- Combine with another Clark attraction for a full day
- Bring a picnic‚Äîthere are nice grounds
- Best for kids old enough to appreciate the cultural aspect

**Time needed:** 1-2 hours

## Tips for Families in Angeles

1. **Beat the heat** - Plan outdoor activities for morning or late afternoon
2. **Bring water everywhere** - Dehydration is real
3. **Mall hopping is valid** - Air conditioning + activities = happy family
4. **Sunday is family day** - Everything is busier; plan accordingly
5. **Tricycles with kids** - Use Grab for longer trips; tricycles work for short hops but aren't the safest
