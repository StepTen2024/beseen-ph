## Overview
Drive organic traffic through locally-focused, SEO-optimized articles that position BE SEEN.PH as THE source for discovering Angeles City, Clark, and Pampanga.

## Target Audience
- Expats living in Angeles/Clark area
- Tourists visiting (domestic & international)
- Koreans (huge community in Clark)
- Filipinos exploring their own backyard
- Business travelers staying at Clark hotels

## Content Calendar Approach
- **Weekly:** 2-3 new articles
- **Monthly Themes:** Align with local events, seasons
- **Evergreen Focus:** 80% evergreen, 20% timely/seasonal

## SEO Strategy
- Long-tail keywords: "best [food] in [location]"
- Location modifiers: Angeles City, Clark, Pampanga, barangay names
- Question formats: "Where to find...", "Is it worth..."
- Comparison articles: "X vs Y"

## Content Quality Standards
- Minimum 1000 words for main guides
- Include specific business names and addresses
- Personal experience tone
- Updated pricing when possible
- Include practical info (hours, location, parking)
- Photos of actual locations (future)

## Conversion Points
- CTA: "Add this place to BE SEEN" for user submissions
- CTA: "Is this your business? Claim it" for owners
- Related business listings at bottom of articles
- Newsletter signup for "more hidden gems"
